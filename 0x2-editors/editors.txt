0. to open a file: Ctrl +x, CTRL+f
1. to save a file: Ctrl+x Ctrl+s
2. to cut an entire line: Ctrl+k
3. to paste: Ctrl+y
5. to undo: Ctrl+/ u
6. to quit Emacs Ctrl+x Ctrl+c
Vi
7. to insert text before the cursor: "I" 
   after the cursor: "A"
8. to delete: "dd"
   cut the current line: "dd"
10. to undo what you just did: "u"
11. to quit vi even though latest changes have not been saved for this vi call: ":q!"
12. to move the cursor to the start of the current line: "0"
13. to move the cursor to the end of the line: "$"
